## Summary

Internal survey platform to embed surveys into product flows, capture feedback in real time, and analyze sentiment trends to surface product issues.

## Problem

No integrated way to collect structured feedback directly within product flows; existing solutions were external and disjointed.

## Goal

Embed surveys, capture user feedback at key journey points, analyze sentiment, and surface recurring issues.

## Responsibilities

- Developed survey UI components, embedding capability, and feedback pipelines; contributed to sentiment analysis.

## Capabilities

- Multiple question types (thumbs, star ratings, numeric, open text) and embedding triggers (post-purchase, post-sign-in, feature completion).

## Impact

- Improved product feedback loops and helped product teams identify key user pain points.
