## Summary

Proof-of-concept to evaluate and integrate BrowserStack for native testing, automation, and multi-device/browser coverage.

## Before

- Device testing relied on physical devices; slow and unscalable
- Tests took days-to-weeks for multiple engineers

## Solution & POC

- Collaborated with checkout team to evaluate market options; selected BrowserStack
- Automated signin flows across device/browser combos and reduced manual effort
- Initially automated WebAuthn tests and progressively added other scenarios

## Outcome

- POC succeeded and BrowserStack was adopted, improving velocity and confidence in releases
