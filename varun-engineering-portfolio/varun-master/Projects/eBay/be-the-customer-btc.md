## Summary

Internal dogfooding platform to link employee enterprise accounts to marketplace accounts, track buying/selling activity, and encourage employee engagement via competitions.

## Problem

Many engineers and product owners were not regular marketplace users, reducing product empathy and limiting real-world feedback.

## Goal

Create an internal platform that links accounts, tracks activity, encourages usage, and introduces friendly competitions.

## Responsibilities

- Learned eBay’s internal UI stack quickly and implemented the frontend with Marko.
- Built data aggregation logic, dashboards for engagement visualization, and a configurable competition engine.

## Technical implementation

### Account Linking
- Connected employee enterprise accounts with marketplace accounts to track purchases, sales, revenue, and engagement.

### Competition Engine
- Configurable competition rules (duration, scoring, points for purchases/sales, leaderboard metrics).

### Data Visualization
- Dashboards for individual activity, team leaderboards, competition rankings, and engagement metrics.

## Impact

- Increased internal dogfooding, improved product empathy, and provided actionable insights to leadership.

## Leadership signals

- Delivered a production project within first 3 months at eBay and rapidly learned a new frontend stack.
