## Summary

Engineered a NodeJS module to determine passkey registration eligibility for a user/browser combination, enabling a smoother return experience.

## Timeline

Stretch work: April — May 2025

## Highlights

- Built a lightweight eligibility-check service used by sign-in and registration flows.
- Delivered a POC that integrated with the sign-in flow within days to validate compatibility and UX.
- Focused on accurate browser feature detection, telemetry, and safe fallbacks to password flows.

## Outcomes & metrics

- Reduced PDC from ~6 months to ~2 weeks for targeted experiments.
- Passkey sign-in success rate observed at 98% in trials, vs ~70% for traditional password sign-ins.
