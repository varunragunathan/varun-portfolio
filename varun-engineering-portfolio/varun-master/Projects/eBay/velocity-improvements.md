## Summary

Led a velocity initiative across four UI applications to remove manual rollout steps, simplify release branches, and automate end-to-end deployments.

## Timeline

October 2024 — present

## Before

- Manual rollouts taking 2–4 PD days per application
- Release-branch system causing chaos and slowdowns
- Frequent human errors and stressed rollouts; delayed rollbacks

## After

- Each commit is automatically pushed towards production with zero manual steps
- Commit-to-first-box under 90 minutes for 95% of commits
- Full production rollout under 120 minutes for 90% of rollouts
- Improved developer experience and reliability; zero rollbacks in two years

## Impact

Significant improvements in delivery speed and engineering morale; measurable reduction in manual toil.
